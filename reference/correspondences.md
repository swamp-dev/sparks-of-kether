## 4. Four Worlds → suits → realms

The Kabbalistic Four Worlds are nested levels of reality. They map onto the
four tarot suits and the four classical elements.

| World | Hebrew | Level | Element | Suit | Realm |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Atziluth** | אצילות | Emanation | Fire | Wands | Divine archetype — pure will |
| **Briah** | בריאה | Creation | Water | Cups | Archangelic — form-ideas, emotion |
| **Yetzirah** | יצירה | Formation | Air | Swords | Angelic — thought, pattern |
| **Assiah** | עשייה | Action | Earth | Pentacles | Manifest — body, matter |

The Tree of Life exists once in each World, so the full map is *four* Trees
stacked — Malkuth of one World becomes Kether of the World below. Sparks of
Kether plays on a single Tree (typically Yetzirah / Formation), but the Four
Worlds structure explains why suits matter if Minor Arcana are ever added.

## 5. Sefirot → Shell keyword (inversions)

Each Sefirah's gift has a shadow. The Shells invert the gift; clearing the
Sefirah's challenge (earning its Spark) banishes its Shell. Full mechanics
in `../design/shells.md`.
